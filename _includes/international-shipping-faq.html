Shipping outside Japan:
  • Orders ship within 2 weeks.
  • Shipping takes about 3-5 days to most countries, but it will depend a lot on how long it takes the package to pass through customs.
  • When your order ships, you will be notified via email. This email will include your tracking information.
  • Signature will be required for delivery.
  • Shipments are fulfilled by EMS and fully insured.
  • You must pay duty and import tax when the package is delivered.
